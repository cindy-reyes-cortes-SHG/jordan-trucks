Asset Management

Jordan Truck Sales is your one stop shop for your asset storage and recovery services. With 37 Years of experience in the industry we know how to manage your assets effectively from start to finish ending with getting your equipment sold for premium dollars.

Transportation

We are equipped with tractors, wrecker booms, and lowboy trailers to recover or transport your equipment anywhere in the United States. We use our own equipment and our own experienced team of trained professionals.

Storage

Our facility encompasses approximately 40 acres of totally fenced, paved, and graveled area with a capacity of 1900 tractors. This provides secure storage of your assets while allowing convenient access for inspections and audits.

Reconditioning

We have a state of the art fourteen bay service and detail facility. We can handle any mechanical repairs as well as cosmetic reconditioning. In addition, we have our own paint booth and excellent paint and body specialists to make your equipment look great. We provide estimates prior to any work being done and will let you decide to whether to wholesale the unit as-is or recondition it for premium retail.

Selling

Our main priority is to get you the most money and to sell your equipment in a timely and orderly fashion. Our experienced sales team will handle all remarketing from advertising, arranging financing, and closing the sale.

References provided upon request. We look forward to having the opportunity to manage your assets and let you focus on what you do best!

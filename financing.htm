Financing

Our Finance Department, led by Jennifer Hancock, is ready to help you into your next truck. We are partnered with the best truck lenders in the industry and can offer competitive financing for all credit types. We also provide in-house finance options to get you into your truck.

Contact one of our associates to assist you through the application process and get you approved.

Jordan Truck Sales works closely with many finance companies. We have a dedicated full time finance manager who looks at each application individually to provide the best financing options for you.

Our In-House Finance Program is designed for customers that may have experienced unfortunate circumstances that have lowered their credit rating. Our contracts are simple-interest loans with no hidden fees or fine print surprises.

There are no balloon payments or purchase options at the end of the loan like you will see with some of the lease or rental programs out there. We design our payment structure to offer affordable monthly payments but yet keeping your payoff balance in line with the value of your truck.

Be sure to talk with us about how this option can open doors that had been previously closed for you.

Welcome To


Jordan Truck Sales

Jordan Truck Sales is a used truck and trailer dealership based in Carrollton, Georgia. We specialize in Peterbilt, Kenworth, Freightliner, Western Star, International, Mack, and Volvo Trucks.

We support our customers throughout the buying process and beyond. That’s why, in addition to used trucks and trailers, JTS offers financing and asset management services. Jordan Truck Capital is our own in-house finance program that can set you up with a simple-interest loan. We also have tractors, wrecker booms, and lowboy trailers for recovery and transport, space on our 38-acre lot for secure asset storage, facilities for truck reconditioning, and much more. Give us a call today and we’ll go to work for you.
